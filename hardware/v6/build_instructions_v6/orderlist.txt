NOTE: ALPHASTAT has a minimum order of 50 EUR. But you can also use other 
types of potentiometers. The holes in the mainboard are for thru-hole mounting 
of Monacor Alpha-Pots (available from www.kessler-elektronik.de) for 1,20 EUR 
each. You need TWO screw-nuts per potentiometer so dont forget to order them. 
On the switchboard there are holes for Conrad-Pots (you have to cut the
plastic-shaft to the desired length). If your potentiometers do not fit in the
holes you can mount them directly to the frontplate or a separate aluminium-
plate which is mounted behind the frontplate.
